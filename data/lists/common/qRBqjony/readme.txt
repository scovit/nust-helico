# TITLE
Genes regulated by small RNAs

# DESCRIPTION
List of genes whose expression is regulated by at least one small RNA according to the Regulon DB database 7.2 (interactions supported by literature with  experimental evidence).

# REFERENCES 
Gama-Castro S et al. RegulonDB (version 7.0): transcriptional regulation of Escherichia coli K-12 integrated within genetic sensory response units (Gensor Units). Nucleic Acids Res. 2011;39(Database issue):D98-105.
