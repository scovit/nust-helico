# TITLE
Genes putatively acquired by horizontal gene transfer.

# DESCRIPTION
Genes predicted to be the result of horizontal gene transfer using a statistical analysis of G+C contents, codon usages, gene positions and amino acid contents of all the genes and derived protein sequences for different organisms. Retrieved from The Horizontal Gene Transfer DataBase (HGT-DB). 

# REFERENCES
Garcia-Vallve S et al. HGT-DB: a database of putative horizontally transferred genes in prokaryotic complete genomes. Nucleic Acids Res. 2003;31(1):187-9. Garcia-Vallve et al. (2003) Horizontal gene transfer database (HGT-DB) at URL: http://genomes.urv.es/HGT-DB/ .
